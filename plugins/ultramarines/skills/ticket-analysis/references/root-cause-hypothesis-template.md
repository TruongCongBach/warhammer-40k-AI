# Root Cause Hypothesis Template

## Purpose

Use this template to compare plausible explanations without collapsing too early onto one answer.

## Template

```markdown
Likely root causes

1. Hypothesis: ...
- Why it fits:
  - ...
- Evidence against or missing:
  - ...
- Likely affected areas:
  - ...
- Fastest confirmation check:
  - ...
- Confidence: high | medium | low

2. Hypothesis: ...
- Why it fits:
  - ...
- Evidence against or missing:
  - ...
- Likely affected areas:
  - ...
- Fastest confirmation check:
  - ...
- Confidence: high | medium | low
```

## Rules

- Keep 2-4 hypotheses unless evidence is already overwhelming.
- Prefer hypotheses that explain several symptoms at once.
- Call out the fastest disambiguation step for each hypothesis.
- Do not convert a hypothesis into a confirmed root cause unless the evidence supports it.
