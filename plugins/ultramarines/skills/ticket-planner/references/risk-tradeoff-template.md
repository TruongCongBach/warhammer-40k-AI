# Risk / Tradeoff Template

## Purpose

Use this template to compare options and make the recommendation defensible.

## Template

```markdown
Risks / tradeoffs

Approach A
- Main benefit: ...
- Main risk: ...
- Regression surface: low | medium | high
- Complexity impact: low | medium | high
- Confidence: high | medium | low

Approach B
- Main benefit: ...
- Main risk: ...
- Regression surface: low | medium | high
- Complexity impact: low | medium | high
- Confidence: high | medium | low

Recommendation basis
- Why A over B: ...
- What could change the decision: ...
```

## Common Risk Categories

- state synchronization risk
- navigation or route transition risk
- API contract mismatch risk
- caching or stale-data risk
- platform-specific UI behavior risk
- auth or permission risk
- rollout or migration risk
- analytics or observability regression risk
